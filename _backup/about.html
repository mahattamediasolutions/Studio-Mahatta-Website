About

A studio built on the belief that stories shape culture — and that craft is never optional.

Studio Mahatta was founded in New Delhi in 2018 with a single conviction: that the stories we choose to tell — and how we tell them — define who we are as a society.

We have produced documentaries for institutions and broadcasters, working across subjects as varied as Himalayan ecology, artisan craft traditions, and urban migration. Our films have screened at national and international platforms.

Today, we are expanding our remit — into long-format storytelling for streaming platforms, into animation, and into original IP development that places Indian stories in a global context.

We are not a large studio. We are a small team that cares deeply — about every frame, every word, every choice. That is our advantage.
